Employee Leave Policy
=====================

1. Annual Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 7 days in advance.
- Managers approve or reject within 3 working days.

2. Sick Leave:
- Up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year with medical certificate for more than 2 days.
- Notify manager as soon as possible.

3. Work from Home:
- Employees may request work from home for up to 10 days/month with manager approval.
- Maintain regular availability during working hours.

4. Benefits:
- Health insurance provided for employee and immediate family.
- Provident fund and tax benefits as per company policy.

Contact HR for more details.
